KMG World Tour 2014 with Eyal Yanilov

KMG 2014 World Tour - Info

Eyal Yanilov will be celebrating 40 Years of teaching and training Krav Maga by embarking upon a World Tour of seminars and workshops, spreading the Krav Maga Global system to students and instructors across the globe.

The Singapore event will be held at YWCA Fort Canning Lodge Banquet Hall. Tickets are expected to sell out quickly for this once in a life time event.

Special commemorative pins and certificationswill be given for every participant of this event.

The year 2014 holds an array of important milestones marking decades of Krav Maga history:

  • 20 years (1994) since Eyal began spreading Krav Maga in Europe. To date, KMG operates in over 30 countries in this continent alone (and over 60 countries worldwide).
  • 30 years (1984) since Eyal transformed Krav Maga from a collections of techniques into an organized system, amission set upon him by Imi.
  • 40 years (1974) since Eyal began training Krav Maga with Imi.
  • 50 years (1964) since Imi retired from his Military service and adapted Krav Maga to the civil life.
  • 70 years (1944) since Imi began teaching Krav Maga to the Jewish "Hagana" resistance organization.
  • 90 Years (1924) since Imi began training boxing and wrestling.

This event are sponsored by Whee - Krav Maga Culture Singapore
